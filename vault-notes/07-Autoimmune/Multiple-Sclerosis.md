# Multiple Sclerosis

> Multiple sclerosis (MS) is an autoimmune disease where T cells attack the myelin sheath of nerve cells in the CNS.

## Key facts

- Symptoms vary by lesion location — vision loss, weakness, numbness, fatigue.
- Strong **vitamin D and EBV (Epstein-Barr virus)** associations — emerging causal evidence.
- More common at high latitudes — sunlight/vit-D angle.
- Modern therapies (e.g., ocrelizumab) deplete B cells, dramatically slowing progression.
