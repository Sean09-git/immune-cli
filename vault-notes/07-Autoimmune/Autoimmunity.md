# Autoimmunity

> Autoimmunity is a state where the immune system attacks the body's own tissues, mistaking 'self' for 'non-self'.

## Key facts

- ~80 known autoimmune diseases — collectively affecting ~5–8% of population.
- Women are affected ~2× more than men — sex hormones modulate immunity.
- Caused by failure of **self-tolerance** — thymic selection or peripheral Treg failure.
- Often triggered by infection (molecular mimicry) or environmental stressors in genetically susceptible individuals.
