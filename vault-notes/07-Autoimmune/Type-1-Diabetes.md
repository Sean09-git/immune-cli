# Type 1 Diabetes

> Type 1 diabetes is an autoimmune disease where T cells destroy the insulin-producing beta cells of the pancreas.

## Key facts

- Onset usually in childhood or young adulthood (vs Type 2's adult metabolic origin).
- Key auto-antibodies: **anti-GAD, anti-IA-2, anti-insulin, anti-ZnT8**.
- Genetic risk concentrated in **HLA-DR3 / HLA-DR4** haplotypes.
- Cure goal: regenerate beta cells + halt the autoimmune attack. Stem-cell therapies in trials.
