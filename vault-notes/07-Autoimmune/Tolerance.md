# Tolerance

> Immune tolerance is the failure-mode-avoiding property: the system learns NOT to attack self.

## Key facts

- **Central tolerance** — in thymus (T cells) and bone marrow (B cells): self-reactive cells are deleted.
- **Peripheral tolerance** — Tregs and anergy keep escapees in check.
- Oral tolerance: gut exposure to a protein trains the system to ignore it — relevant for food allergy prevention.
- Pregnancy is a natural masterclass in tolerance — the foetus is 50% non-self yet not rejected.
