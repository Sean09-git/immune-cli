# Atopy

> Atopy is the genetic tendency to develop IgE-mediated allergies — eczema, asthma, hay fever, food allergy.

## Key facts

- Highly heritable: 1 atopic parent → ~30% child risk. 2 atopic parents → ~60%.
- Associated with **Th2-skewed** immunity from early life.
- Filaggrin gene mutations → broken skin barrier → atopic dermatitis pathway.
- Often follows the 'atopic march' — atopic dermatitis → food allergy → asthma → allergic rhinitis.

Family-history field at onboarding → enables risk-aware insight messages.
