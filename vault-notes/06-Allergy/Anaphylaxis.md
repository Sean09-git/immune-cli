# Anaphylaxis

> Anaphylaxis is a severe, systemic allergic reaction that can become fatal within minutes.

## Key facts

- Triggers: foods (peanut, shellfish), insect stings, medications, latex.
- Mechanism: **systemic mast cell degranulation** → massive histamine release → vasodilation → shock.
- Treatment: **intramuscular epinephrine** (EpiPen) — within minutes.
- Biphasic in 5–20% of cases: a second wave hours after the first calms.

## App connection

Severe-reaction flag triggers an emergency card with action steps — never replacement for medical care.
