# Allergy

> When the adaptive immune system mistakes harmless things for enemies.

## Notes in this category

- [[Allergy]] — *An allergy is an immune reaction to a normally harmless substance, mediated mostly by IgE and mast cells.*
- [[Anaphylaxis]] — *Anaphylaxis is a severe, systemic allergic reaction that can become fatal within minutes.*
- [[Histamine]] — *Histamine is a small molecule released by mast cells that causes itch, swelling, and bronchoconstriction.*
- [[Atopy]] — *Atopy is the genetic tendency to develop IgE-mediated allergies — eczema, asthma, hay fever, food allergy.*
- [[Hygiene-Hypothesis]] — *The hygiene hypothesis proposes that reduced early-life microbial exposure increases allergy and autoimmune risk.*
