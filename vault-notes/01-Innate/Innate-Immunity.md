# Innate Immunity

> Innate immunity is the body's inherited, first-line defense — fast, broad, and the same in every person.

## Key facts

- Active within **minutes to hours** of an invader's arrival.
- Does **not** remember — same response every time, even to a familiar threat.
- Three layers: barriers (skin, mucosa), cells (phagocytes, NK), molecules (complement, cytokines).
- Recognises broad patterns called **PAMPs** (pathogen-associated molecular patterns) via receptors like Toll-like receptors (TLRs).

## App connection

Most cold/flu symptoms in the journal are innate immunity at work. Track them and you're literally tracking your innate response.
