# Neutrophil

> A neutrophil is the most abundant white blood cell — a short-lived innate phagocyte that arrives first at infection sites.

## Key facts

- **Most abundant** immune cell in blood (~60% of white blood cells).
- Lifespan: only ~5 days. Cells die *for* the cause.
- Three weapons: phagocytosis, granule release, and **NETs** (Neutrophil Extracellular Traps).
- High count in blood test = often a bacterial infection.

## App connection

Sore throat, pus, redness — neutrophil signatures. Worth tagging in the symptom journal.
