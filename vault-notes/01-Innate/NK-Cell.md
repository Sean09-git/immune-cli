# NK Cell

> An NK (natural killer) cell is an innate lymphocyte that kills virally infected and cancerous cells without needing prior exposure.

## Key facts

- Lymphocyte by lineage, but **innate** by function — no need for antigen training.
- Detects cells that **lack MHC class I** (a sign of viral hijacking or tumour).
- Kills via perforin and granzymes — same toolkit as cytotoxic T cells.
- Releases **IFN-γ**, which activates macrophages.

## App connection

Stress and poor sleep measurably lower NK activity within days — a direct journal-to-immunity link.
