# Innate Immunity

> The body's hardwired first response — fast, generic, and ancient.

## Notes in this category

- [[Innate-Immunity]] — *Innate immunity is the body's inherited, first-line defense — fast, broad, and the same in every person.*
- [[Macrophage]] — *A macrophage is a long-lived innate immune cell that eats pathogens, cleans debris, and signals the rest of the immune system.*
- [[Neutrophil]] — *A neutrophil is the most abundant white blood cell — a short-lived innate phagocyte that arrives first at infection sites.*
- [[NK-Cell]] — *An NK (natural killer) cell is an innate lymphocyte that kills virally infected and cancerous cells without needing prior exposure.*
- [[Complement]] — *The complement system is a cascade of ~30 blood proteins that punch holes in pathogens, tag them for eating, and amplify inflammation.*
