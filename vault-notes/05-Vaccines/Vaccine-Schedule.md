# Vaccine Schedule

> A vaccine schedule is the recommended timeline of vaccinations from infancy through adulthood.

## Key facts

- Most childhood vaccines cluster in **first 18 months** — when adaptive immunity is most trainable.
- Korea's schedule: WHO-aligned, with TB (BCG) at birth, MMR at 12 months, HPV at 11–12 years.
- France's schedule: 11 mandatory vaccines for under-2s since 2018.
- Boosters: tetanus every 10 years, flu annually, COVID per advisory.

## App connection

User imports their schedule → app shows what each shot trains and when next booster is due.
