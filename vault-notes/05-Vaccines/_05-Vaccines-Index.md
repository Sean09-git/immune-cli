# Vaccines

> How we teach the immune system without paying the cost of real disease.

## Notes in this category

- [[Vaccine]] — *A vaccine is a preparation that trains adaptive immunity to recognise a pathogen — without causing the disease.*
- [[mRNA-Vaccine]] — *An mRNA vaccine delivers messenger RNA encoding a viral protein, which cells then make and display.*
- [[Attenuated-Vaccine]] — *An attenuated vaccine uses a weakened but live form of a pathogen to induce a strong, long-lasting response.*
- [[Adjuvant]] — *An adjuvant is a substance added to a vaccine to boost the immune response to the antigen.*
- [[Vaccine-Schedule]] — *A vaccine schedule is the recommended timeline of vaccinations from infancy through adulthood.*
