# Vaccine

> A vaccine is a preparation that trains adaptive immunity to recognise a pathogen — without causing the disease.

## Key facts

- Goal: generate **memory T and B cells** specific to the pathogen.
- **Primary response** to first dose is small. **Booster doses** trigger memory recall — stronger and faster.
- Adjuvants (e.g., aluminium salts) amplify the immune response by mimicking PAMPs.
- Herd immunity threshold: ~95% for measles, ~80% for COVID — varies by R₀ of the pathogen.
