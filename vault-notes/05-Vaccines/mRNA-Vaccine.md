# mRNA Vaccine

> An mRNA vaccine delivers messenger RNA encoding a viral protein, which cells then make and display.

## Key facts

- First approved mRNA vaccines: Pfizer & Moderna COVID-19 (2020).
- Lipid nanoparticles (LNPs) deliver the mRNA into cells.
- Your own cells transiently produce the **spike protein** — your immune system trains against it.
- Advantages: fast to design, no live virus. Limits: cold-chain storage, short shelf life.
