# IgE

> IgE is the antibody class that drives allergic reactions by arming mast cells.

## Key facts

- Least abundant antibody in blood (~0.05%) — but most consequential per molecule.
- Binds tightly to **FcεRI receptors** on mast cells and basophils.
- Class switching to IgE requires **IL-4** from Th2 cells.
- Originally evolved against parasites (worms). Modern allergies = misfiring of this arm.

## App connection

IgE level (lab value) + symptom severity (journal) — a key chart for allergy users.
