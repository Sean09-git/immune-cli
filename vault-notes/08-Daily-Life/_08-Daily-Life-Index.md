# Immunity in Daily Life

> The everyday inputs you can actually track — and which are the highest-yield app signals.

## Notes in this category

- [[Sleep-and-Immunity]] — *Sleep, especially deep slow-wave sleep, consolidates immune memory and recalibrates inflammation.*
- [[Stress-and-Immunity]] — *Stress modulates immunity through the HPA axis — cortisol suppresses lymphocytes while shifting cytokine balance.*
- [[Exercise-and-Immunity]] — *Exercise has a J-shaped dose-response with immunity — moderate boosts it, extreme exhausts it.*
- [[Gut-Microbiome]] — *The gut microbiome is the trillions of bacteria, archaea, fungi, and viruses living in the intestines — central to immune calibration.*
- [[Sunlight-Vitamin-D]] — *Sunlight on skin synthesises vitamin D, which directly regulates immune cell function — modulating both innate and adaptive arms.*
