# Sunlight Vitamin D

> Sunlight on skin synthesises vitamin D, which directly regulates immune cell function — modulating both innate and adaptive arms.

## Key facts

- Vitamin D receptors are on T cells, B cells, macrophages, and dendritic cells.
- Low vit D associated with higher autoimmune (MS, Type 1 diabetes) and respiratory infection rates.
- 10–30 min midday sun (face + arms) several times/week is usually sufficient for skin synthesis.
- Latitude > 35° = limited UVB in winter — supplementation often needed.

## App connection

Sun-exposure log + lat/long → seasonal vit-D risk warnings (e.g., 'Paris winter, consider checking levels').
