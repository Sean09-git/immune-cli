# Sleep and Immunity

> Sleep, especially deep slow-wave sleep, consolidates immune memory and recalibrates inflammation.

## Key facts

- During sleep: **growth hormone** rises, **cortisol** drops, T-cell migration into lymph nodes peaks.
- <6h sleep → measurably weaker vaccine response (lower antibody titres for flu and hepatitis A/B).
- Chronic sleep loss elevates **IL-6, TNF-α, CRP** — markers of chronic inflammation.
- Even one bad night reduces NK cell cytotoxicity by ~70%.

## App connection

Daily sleep input is the **highest-yield single feature** — connects to almost every other variable.
