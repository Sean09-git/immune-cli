# Stress and Immunity

> Stress modulates immunity through the HPA axis — cortisol suppresses lymphocytes while shifting cytokine balance.

## Key facts

- **Acute stress** (minutes–hours): sharpens innate immunity, helpful short-term.
- **Chronic stress** (weeks+): suppresses adaptive immunity, slows wound healing, reactivates dormant viruses (HSV, EBV).
- Pathway: stress → hypothalamus → cortisol → lymphocyte suppression + Th1→Th2 shift.
- Mindfulness and exercise can buffer the immune cost of stress.
