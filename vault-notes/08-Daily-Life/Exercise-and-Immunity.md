# Exercise and Immunity

> Exercise has a J-shaped dose-response with immunity — moderate boosts it, extreme exhausts it.

## Key facts

- Moderate exercise (30–60 min): mobilises NK cells, T cells, neutrophils into circulation.
- Each session is a **mini-immune patrol** — re-circulating cells through tissues.
- Excessive endurance (marathon, ultra) → 3–72h **open window** of suppressed immunity.
- Long-term: physically active people have lower inflammation markers and better vaccine responses.

## App connection

Exercise log → 'cells mobilised' visualisation. Gamify steady moderate activity.
