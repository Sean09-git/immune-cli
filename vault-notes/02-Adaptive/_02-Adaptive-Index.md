# Adaptive Immunity

> The learned, tailored, and remembered immune response — the reason vaccines work.

## Notes in this category

- [[Adaptive-Immunity]] — *Adaptive immunity is the body's tailored defense — slow at first, but precise, and it remembers.*
- [[Helper-T-Cell]] — *A helper T cell (CD4+) is the orchestrator of the adaptive immune response — it activates B cells and macrophages.*
- [[Cytotoxic-T-Cell]] — *A cytotoxic T cell (CD8+) is the targeted killer of the adaptive system — it destroys infected or cancerous cells directly.*
- [[B-Cell]] — *A B cell is the antibody-producing arm of adaptive immunity — it becomes either a plasma cell or a memory cell.*
- [[Memory-Cell]] — *A memory cell is a long-lived T or B cell that 'remembers' a past antigen and responds explosively on re-exposure.*
