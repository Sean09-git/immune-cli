# Adaptive Immunity

> Adaptive immunity is the body's tailored defense — slow at first, but precise, and it remembers.

## Key facts

- Two main cell types: **T cells** (cellular immunity) and **B cells** (humoral immunity).
- First exposure: response takes **7–14 days**. Second exposure: hours.
- Recognises specific molecular shapes — **antigens** — via receptors built by random gene shuffling (V(D)J recombination).
- Generates **memory cells** that live for years to decades.

## App connection

Every vaccine in your record is a successful adaptive immune training session — worth surfacing in the timeline.
