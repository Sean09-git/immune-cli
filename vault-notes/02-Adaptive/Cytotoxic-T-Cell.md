# Cytotoxic T Cell

> A cytotoxic T cell (CD8+) is the targeted killer of the adaptive system — it destroys infected or cancerous cells directly.

## Key facts

- Recognises antigens on **MHC class I**, which every nucleated cell carries.
- Kills via **perforin** (pore-forming) and **granzymes** (apoptosis triggers).
- Crucial against intracellular pathogens (viruses) and tumours.
- Cancer immunotherapy (checkpoint inhibitors) basically unleashes CD8+ T cells.

## App connection

Recovering from a viral infection? Your CD8+ T cells did most of the cleanup.
