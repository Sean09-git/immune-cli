# Regulatory T Cell

> A regulatory T cell (Treg) is a subtype that brakes the immune response — preventing autoimmunity.

## Key facts

- Marker: **FOXP3** transcription factor.
- Suppresses other T cells by secreting **IL-10** and **TGF-β**, or by direct contact.
- Treg failure → autoimmune disease (Type 1 diabetes, IPEX syndrome).
- Cancer cells often recruit Tregs to hide from the immune system.
